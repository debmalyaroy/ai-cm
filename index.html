1. Why AI is Required

The Linguistic Barrier

Rule-based NLP requires rigid dictionary mapping. AI handles spelling, jargon, and intent weight instinctively, ensuring managers don't need technical query knowledge.

AI Advantage: Native semantic intent isolation handles spelling and business jargon instinctively.

Dynamic SQL Self-Healing

Mapping to 15+ tables via templates is brittle. Our Analyst agent uses a ReAct loop to interpret schema errors and self-correct SQL on-the-fly without developer intervention.

AI Advantage: Autonomous navigation of complex star-schema relational data.
